Introduction

Drug consumption among students is increasing at an alarming rate and the newer forms of drugs reach students It is important to find the ways and forms in which drug reaches students. Drug mafia trap students at the school premises and abuse them. Unfortunately the several awareness campaigns and deaddiction programmes run by the government, school authorities, and NGOs do not reach the target audience. Their anti-drug effort, energy and resources, in anti-drug campaign activities is yet to reach the desired outcome.

Read More

The Kasaragod District Panchayath has been worried about the situation and want to take effective steps to counter the menace of drug consumption among students. It approached Kerala Start up Mission and the Central University of Kerala to conduct a detailed study and identify the root causes for the increasing drug consumption among students. It also wants to explore if technology can be put into use to resisting this.

Department of Public Administration, Central University of Kerala has took up the challenge to conduct a survey in Kasaragod at 15 different schools in different locations, varied categories such as government, aided, private. Approxely1500 students, 500 teachers, and 500 parents were part of the survey.

On the basis of this analysis, we are inviting Technology solutions to reduce drug consumption among students and enable students themselves in this anti-drug campaign effectively. Details of the requirement are also published in this website as problem statement.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Technology solution to report drug usage or deals and create awareness

A technology solution to report consumption and dealing of drugs near campuses by keeping the anonymity of the reporting person.

Address the issue of rare chances of a technology solution being used just to report drug consumption. There should be some engaging contents or rewards or recognition, encouraging the user to adopt the solution.

A Solution which can communicate all relevant information including important such Contact details ,Department details whichever you feel relevant.

This should be interactive in such a way that enables discussion/queries related to drug abuse, keeping the identity of the participants of the discussion anonymous.

Facilitate sharing interesting contents in social media.

Rating or honoring by giving badge to those who are posting relevant contents at frequent intervals against drug usage.

Engage NGOs / institutions so that they can run campaign, competitions against drug usage.

A platform for identifying the behavioral changes after the consumption through emotion recognition.

One or a combination of the above mentioned points or any other technological solutions which can be effectively deployed to address the issue of drug abuse.
